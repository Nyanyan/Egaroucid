Play against Edax4.4

Edax 4.4 is one of the best Othello AI in the world.If I set the game from the very beginning, same line appears a lot. To avoid this, I set the game from many different near-draw lines.No opening books used.If Egaroucid Win Ratio is over 0.5, then Egaroucid wins more than Edax do.I used XOT for its testcases.
